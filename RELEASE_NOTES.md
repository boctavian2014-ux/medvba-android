### ✨ Key Features

#### 📚 Comprehensive Quiz System
- **30,000+ Medical Questions** covering all major anatomy systems
- **Multiple Quiz Modes:**
  - Quick Quiz: 10 questions for rapid practice
  - Practice Session: 25 questions with instant feedback
  - Exam Simulation: 100 questions with realistic exam conditions
- **Categories Include:**
  - Upper/Lower Limbs (Bones, Muscles, Joints, Vessels, Nerves)
  - Internal Organs (Respiratory, Cardiovascular, Digestive, Urinary, Reproductive)
  - Head & Neck (Skull, Face, Brain, Sensory Organs)
  - Neuroanatomy (CNS, PNS, Brain Regions, Vascular Supply)
- **Instant Feedback:** Detailed explanations for every question
- **Progress Tracking:** Monitor accuracy and completion rates per category

#### 🤖 AI Medical Tutor
- **24/7 Availability:** Get instant answers to medical questions
- **Expert Knowledge:** Covers Anatomy, Physiology, Pathology, Pharmacology, and more
- **Clinical Correlations:** Learn with real-world medical applications
- **Structured Explanations:** Clear, comprehensive answers with mnemonics
- **Free Plan:** 1 AI question per day
- **Premium:** Unlimited AI tutor access

- **Virtual Study Sessions:** Join or host live study rooms with Zoom integration
- **Real-Time Collaboration:** Study with fellow medical students worldwide
- **Schedule Sessions:** Plan future study sessions with automatic reminders

### 📋 Permissions Explained

When using MEDVBA, we may request the following permissions:

- **Notifications (Optional):**
  - Purpose: Remind you about scheduled study sessions and daily streaks
  - You can enable/disable this in Settings

- **Camera & Microphone (Via Zoom):**
  - Purpose: When joining live study rooms, Zoom will request camera/microphone access for video calls
  - These permissions are handled by the Zoom app, not directly by MEDVBA
  - You can join study rooms audio-only if you prefer
